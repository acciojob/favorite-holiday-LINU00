Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Known as the "City of Light", it is famous for its art, culture, history, and breathtaking landmarks.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A beautiful avenue for shopping and sightseeing.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Offering stunning views of the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicious French cookies.
  • French Onion Soup – A warm and comforting dish.
  • Baguette – Classic French bread with a crispy crust.
